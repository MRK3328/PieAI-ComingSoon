Mission

At Pie AI, our mission is to revolutionize the restaurant industry through AI-driven innovation, ensuring a safer and more seamless dining experience for all. We are committed to enhancing food safety and accessibility, particularly for individuals with allergies, by providing real-time, accurate menu information for restaurants. Pie AI reduces operational strain by streamlining customer interactions, minimizing reliance on servers and managers for routine inquiries. By optimizing efficiency and enhancing safety, we empower businesses to deliver exceptional service while creating a more inclusive and effortless dining experience for every guest.

The Beginning

Pie AI started as a simple idea — born from firsthand experience in the restaurant industry. I began working at the same local spot when I was 15, starting as a busser. Over time, I worked my way up — through serving and eventually to bartender. I saw the challenges restaurants face in ensuring allergy safety for customers. Servers often struggled to provide accurate allergen information, creating risks for both diners and businesses.

With a background in business and a passion for problem-solving, I set out to build a solution. Teaching myself to code and mastering AI technology, I developed Pie AI from the ground up — testing, refining, and optimizing it along the way. Today, Pie AI stands as an innovative tool designed to enhance restaurant efficiency, improve customer safety, and streamline dining experiences for all.

Dining out should always feel safe !

Every year, thousands of people experience allergic reactions in restaurants often because of small, preventable mistakes. Pie AI empowers restaurants to update menus in real time, communicate with clarity, and safeguard every guest, one meal at a time
