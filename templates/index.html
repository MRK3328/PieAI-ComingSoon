How it works

Pie AI is a smart assistant for restaurants, designed to enhance the dining experience. It learns your menu, specials, and allergy info — then answers customer questions instantly.

Update items in real time, and Pie AI adjusts on the spot. No downloads. No extra hardware. Just fast, accurate answers powered by your own restaurant.

Restaurant Benefits

Pie AI transforms the dining experience by providing instant access to restaurant information — no additional technology required. A simple QR code at the table allows customers to scan and instantly view menus, specials, allergy details, and more.

Pie AI enhances, not replaces, the role of servers. Guests can quickly get information they need while maintaining a personal touch. It ensures smooth communication during high-traffic hours or special events.

Each restaurant’s AI learns customer habits and common questions, automatically improving accuracy and reducing staff workload.

Managers can update daily specials or out-of-stock items instantly, ensuring real-time accuracy across all customer interactions.

Customer Assistant

Pie AI provides instant access to restaurant details via QR code — no app downloads or logins required. The system makes dining effortless and modern.

Guests can interact directly with Pie AI to ask about menu items, specials, or allergens without waiting for staff availability.

Customers can ask about allergens in real time. The AI helps reduce miscommunication, ensuring every meal is safe and personalized.

The experience is simple and intuitive for all guests — no installation needed. Pie AI blends technology with hospitality.
