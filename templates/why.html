Every Meal Should Be Safe

Every day, people trust restaurants with their health — yet food allergies continue to cause thousands of emergency visits and hundreds of deaths each year.

Many of these incidents happen simply because staff don’t have fast, reliable access to accurate allergen information. Pie AI was created to fix this.

Our goal is to make dining safer, smarter, and more human — helping restaurants communicate clearly, update menus in real time, and protect customers from preventable mistakes.
